1.2 Plot Student Enrollment by Semester and Institution

Design Choices

For this task, I have chosen to create a grouped bar chart to visualize Student Enrollment by Semester and Institution over multiple semesters. Here are the design choices that led me to select this type of chart:

  • Grouped Bar Chart: I opted for a grouped bar chart because it is well-suited for comparing data across multiple categories (institutions) over time (semesters). Each inst-itution will have its own set of bars, making it easy to see how enrollment levels change for each one.

  • Color Differentiation: I used different colors for each institution's bars in the chart. This color differentiation makes it visually clear which bars correspond to which institution, enhancing readability and aiding in trend identification.

  • Labels: I included labels for both the x-axis (semesters) and the y-axis (enrollment). This ensures that viewers can easily understand the data and its context without needing additional information.

Trends Identified

Analyzing the grouped bar chart, several trends become apparent:

  • UH Manoa Enrollment: Enrollment at UH Manoa shows a declining trend starting from around 2012. However, there is a notable increase in enrollment after 2019, suggesting that the negative impact of the COVID-19 era on enrollments may not be as severe for this institution.

  • West Oahu's Gradual Growth: West Oahu exhibits a gradual and consistent growth trend over the years, indicating that this institution has been steadily attracting more students.

  • Similar Enrollment Declines: Several other institutions seem to be experiencing similar declining enrollment rates. This may be indicative of broader trends affecting these institutions or factors influencing enrollment across multiple institutions.

  • Kaua'i's Consistency: Kaua'i appears to maintain relatively stable enrollment levels throughout the observed period. This suggests that this institution's enrollment has been relatively unaffected by the fluctuations seen in other institutions.

In summary, the grouped bar chart effectively highlights enrollment trends across different institutions and semesters, making it easier to identify patterns and draw insights from the data.
